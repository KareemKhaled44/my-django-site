SHIPPING POLICY

Fast, reliable delivery for your Peak Health supplements

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

We work hard to ensure your supplements arrive quickly and in perfect condition. Below you'll find all the details about our shipping process.

Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST Monday-Friday are processed the same day
  • Weekend and holiday orders are processed the next business day
  • During peak seasons, processing may take 1-2 additional business days

Note:

Processing time is separate from shipping time and does not include weekends or holidays.

Shipping Methods & Rates

Service Delivery Time Price
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days $5.99

Overnight Shipping 1 business day $24.99
Free Shipping 5-7 business days Free on orders over $50

Delivery Areas

  • We ship to all 50 U.S. states
  • International shipping available to select countries
  • Some remote areas may experience longer delivery times

International Orders:

Additional customs fees, taxes, or duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

  • You'll receive a tracking number via email when your order ships
  • Tracking updates typically appear within 24 hours of shipment
  • Contact us if your tracking hasn't updated after 3 business days

Track your order through our website or directly with the carrier.

Undeliverable Packages

Packages may be returned to us as undeliverable for several reasons:

  • Incorrect or incomplete address
  • Failed delivery attempts
  • Customs refusal

Address Accuracy:

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We're not responsible for packages shipped to incorrect addresses provided by customers.
