RETURN POLICY

Your satisfaction is our priority at Peak Health

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At Peak Health Supplements, we stand behind our products. If you're not completely satisfied, please review our return policy below.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return:

  • Items must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Return request must be made within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase is required

Note on Opened Products:

For quality control reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened supplement containers unless the product is defective.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened supplement containers
  • Products purchased during clearance sales
  • Personalized or special order items
  • Products without original packaging
  • Items damaged due to misuse

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team at returns@peakhealth.com within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for approval and return instructions
  4. Ship items back using the provided return label (customer responsible for return shipping)
  5. Receive refund to original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect your return

Important:

We recommend using a trackable shipping service as we cannot guarantee receipt of your return.

Refunds

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days after we receive your return
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Restocking fees may apply for large quantity returns

Sale items are only eligible for store credit.

Exchanges

We currently only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange for a different product:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the desired product

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@peakhealth.com
  • Include photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We'll ship a replacement at no additional cost

For perishable items, we may require return of the damaged product.

Return Questions?

Contact our customer service team for assistance with returns:

Email: returns@peakhealth.com

Phone: 1-800-PEAK-HEALTH (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm EST)

Mail: Peak Health Returns, 123 Supplement Lane, Health City, HC 12345
