PRIVACY POLICY

Your trust is important to us at Peak Health

At Peak Health Supplements, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, shipping/billing address
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through our payment processor)
  • Health Information: Optional fitness goals or supplement preferences you share with us
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Process and fulfill your supplement orders
  • Provide personalized product recommendations
  • Improve our products and customer service
  • Send important account/order notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure payment processing with PCI-compliant partners
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data

While we take reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Enable shopping cart functionality
  • Deliver targeted ads (through third-party services)

Cookie Consent

By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies. You can manage preferences in your browser settings.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a regulatory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@peakhealth.com

Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. The updated version will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.

For material changes, we may notify you via email or through our website.
